# Eazzio Books - User Manual

This user manual explains how to use the Eazzio Books accounting system.

---

## 1. Introduction

Eazzio Books is an accounting management system designed to manage daily business operations such as customers, vendors, items, quotes, invoices, payments, expenses, purchase orders, and financial reports.

---

## 2. Login

### Steps

1. Open the Eazzio Books web application.
2. Enter registered email and password.
3. Click on Login.
4. After successful login, the user will be redirected to the dashboard.

---

## 3. Dashboard

The dashboard shows a summary of business activities.

It may include:

- Total receivables
- Total payables
- Monthly income
- Monthly expenses
- Profit summary
- Projected payments
- Recent invoices
- Recent expenses

Use the dashboard to quickly understand the current financial position of the business.

---

## 4. Customer Management

### Add Customer

1. Go to Customers.
2. Click Add Customer.
3. Enter customer name, email, phone, GST number, and address.
4. Click Save.

### View Customer

1. Go to Customers.
2. Select a customer from the list.
3. View customer details and transactions.

### Edit Customer

1. Open customer details.
2. Click Edit.
3. Update required details.
4. Click Save.

---

## 5. Vendor Management

### Add Vendor

1. Go to Vendors.
2. Click Add Vendor.
3. Enter vendor name, email, phone, GST number, and address.
4. Click Save.

### View Vendor

1. Go to Vendors.
2. Select a vendor.
3. View vendor details and purchase records.

---

## 6. Item Management

### Add Item

1. Go to Items.
2. Click New Item.
3. Enter item name, type, rate, HSN/SAC code, and unit.
4. Select inventory tracking if the item is stock-based.
5. Click Save.

### Item Types

| Type | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Product | Physical item with quantity |
| Service | Non-stock service item |

---

## 7. Quotes

A quote is an estimated price sent to a customer before creating an invoice.

### Create Quote

1. Go to Quotes.
2. Click New Quote.
3. Select customer.
4. Add items, quantity, rate, tax, and discount.
5. Save the quote.

### Convert Quote

A quote can be converted into:

- Sales Order
- Invoice

---

## 8. Sales Orders

Sales orders are created after a customer confirms a quote.

### Create Sales Order

1. Go to Sales Orders.
2. Click New Sales Order.
3. Select customer.
4. Add items and details.
5. Save the sales order.

A sales order can later be converted into an invoice or delivery challan depending on the business flow.

---

## 9. Invoices

Invoices are created to request payment from customers.

### Create Invoice

1. Go to Invoices.
2. Click New Invoice.
3. Select customer.
4. Add invoice date and due date.
5. Add items.
6. Apply tax or discount if needed.
7. Save invoice.

### Invoice Status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Draft | Invoice is prepared but not finalized |
| Sent | Invoice has been sent to customer |
| Partially Paid | Some amount has been received |
| Paid | Full payment has been received |

---

## 10. Payments Received

This module records payments received from customers.

### Record Payment

1. Go to Payments Received.
2. Click Add Payment.
3. Select customer and invoice.
4. Enter payment amount.
5. Select payment mode.
6. Save payment.

After payment is saved, the invoice balance is updated.

---

## 11. Expenses

Expenses are used to record money spent by the business.

### Add Expense

1. Go to Expenses.
2. Click Add Expense.
3. Select expense category.
4. Enter amount, date, and payment mode.
5. Add vendor if applicable.
6. Save expense.

Examples of expenses:

- Office rent
- Staff salary
- Electricity bill
- Internet bill
- Travel expense
- Software subscription

---

## 12. Purchase Orders

Purchase orders are used to request goods or services from vendors.

### Create Purchase Order

1. Go to Purchase Orders.
2. Click New Purchase Order.
3. Select vendor.
4. Add items.
5. Enter quantity and rate.
6. Save purchase order.

Purchase orders can later be converted into bills if the bill module is available.

---

## 13. Delivery Challans

Delivery challans are used when goods are delivered to the customer.

### Create Delivery Challan

1. Go to Delivery Challans.
2. Click New Delivery Challan.
3. Select customer or sales order.
4. Add items.
5. Save challan.

For inventory-tracked items, stock may reduce after delivery challan generation.

---

## 14. Reports

Reports help analyze the financial condition of the business.

Available reports may include:

- Profit & Loss Report
- Balance Sheet
- Trial Balance
- Cash Flow Report
- Aging Report
- Sales Report
- Purchase Report

### View Report

1. Go to Reports.
2. Select required report.
3. Choose date range if available.
4. View generated report.

---

## 15. Settings

The settings module is used to manage:

- Organization details
- User preferences
- Invoice settings
- Tax settings
- Export or backup options

---

## 16. User Roles

| Role | Access |
|---|---|
| Admin | Full access |
| Accountant | Accounting and reports access |
| Staff | Operational module access |
| Viewer | View-only access |

---

## 17. Best Practices

- Add customers and vendors before creating invoices or purchase orders.
- Keep item details updated.
- Record payments immediately after receiving them.
- Check reports regularly.
- Use proper tax and HSN/SAC details.
- Do not share login credentials.
- Take regular backups of important data.

---

## 18. Conclusion

This user manual provides basic instructions for using Eazzio Books. It helps users manage accounting operations such as customer records, sales, purchases, invoices, expenses, payments, and reports.
